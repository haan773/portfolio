Finding Belonging Again

Overview

'Cultury' addresses the challenge of feeling disconnected from one’s cultural roots when born and raised in a different country. By offering engaging cultural events, cooking experiences, and social connections, it provides a fun and meaningful way to rediscover, embrace, and preserve one’s identity

Team Project

Sema Turan, Ha An Nguyen

Timeline

November 2022 - February 2023

Tools

Adobe XD, InDesign, Illustrator

Problem

Feeling Torn Between Two Cultures

Being caught between two cultures can feel like losing a part of yourself. I've noticed that many people who grow up away from their ancestral homeland struggle with a fading connection to their heritage, making them feel lost or disconnected from their roots.
Solution

Making Cultural Reconnection Fun and Engaging

Simply learning about one’s heritage isn’t enough—it needs to be an engaging and immersive experience. By integrating cultural events, cooking sessions, and social connections, this project helps individuals rediscover their identity in a way that feels natural, fun, and deeply fulfilling.

Reconnect, Experience, Belong

Rediscover your cultural roots through immersive experiences, from food events to cultural education. It’s about reconnecting with tradition, building meaningful relationships, and fostering a true sense of belonging in an ever-evolving world.

Beauty of tradition

Discover a world of traditions through diverse categories like recipes, greetings, traditional clothing, and more. Dive into authentic cuisine by exploring traditional dishes, complete with step-by-step cooking instructions, cooking time, calorie details, and expert tips to perfect your meal
