Redesign

Dominosteine Dennree

Overview

This project focuses on the redesign of Dennree Dominosteine, a traditional German Christmas treat, to create packaging that is both modern and sustainable. Our goal was to make the product more appealing to a younger audience while reducing its environmental impact. By replacing the original plastic packaging with eco-friendly materials, the new design introcduces a fresh, contemporary look that still captures the festive spirit of the holiday season.

Team Project

Noemie Brück, Ha An Nguyen

Timeline

October 2023 - February 2024

Tools

Illustrator, InDesign,Photoshop

Pattern

Since Dominosteine is a classic Christmas treat in Germany, we aimed to capture the festive spirit through a thoughtfully designed pattern, without falling into clichés. This pattern incorporates elements that represent the ingredients and the Dominosteine itself, creating a modern interpretation of the treat's traditional appeal.

Packaging

The packaging is designed to be square, reflecting the shape of the Dominostein, with two versions: standard and luxury. Both feature a loop for easy opening, a protective film for freshness, and removable paper dividers. The luxury edition includes three compartments, reflecting the three layers of the Dominostein, making it ideal for both gifting and elegant storage.
