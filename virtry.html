Overview

'Virtry' is a virtual dressing room app designed for online shoppers seeking a more confident and engaging shopping experience. It helps users visualize how second hand clothes will fit and look on them, reducing uncertainty and promoting sustainable fashion choices. With fun features like mix-and-match styling, Virtry makes online shopping both interactive and eco-friendly.

Problem

The Dilemma of Digital Shopping

Online shoppers often face frustration due to clothes that don’t fit as expected, leading to dissatisfaction and a lack of confidence. Additionally, many want to avoid supporting fast fashion but find it hard to visualize second-hand clothing when shopping online.
Solution

Conscious Shopping Meets Digital Convenience

'Virtry' bridges the gap between conscious shopping and digital convenience with an interactive virtual dressing room. Users can digitally try on clothes for a confident fit. It also promotes sustainable shopping by making second-hand clothing easier to visualize, encouraging eco-friendly fashion choices with fun mix-and-match features.
